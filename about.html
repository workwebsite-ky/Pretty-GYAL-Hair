Our Story

Built From Passion, Powered By Purpose

Pretty GYAL Hair LLC is a luxury hair brand created with the vision of helping women feel confident, beautiful and empowered through high-quality hair. Founded by Treyvione Richardson, Pretty GYAL Hair was built from a passion for beauty, entrepreneurship, and creating an experience beyond just selling hair.

The brand focuses on providing premium human hair while creating a community centered around confidence, self-expression and empowerment. Through luxury products, events and community initiatives, Pretty GYAL Hair continues to redefine the beauty experience.

  • 100% raw virgin hair — high quality, no compromises
  • Brooklyn born, serving NYC and the world
  • Beauty, fashion and entertainment under one crown
